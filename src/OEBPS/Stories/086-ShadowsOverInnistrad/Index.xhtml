Shadows over Innistrad

Seeking revenge on Sorin Markov for what she sees as his abandonment of her home, Zendikar, Nahiri brings the Eldrazi threat to Innistrad. Jace Beleren, realizing what is happening, brings the Gatewatch to help save the plane.
